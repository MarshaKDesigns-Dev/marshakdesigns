My Story

I didn't come into tech through the traditional route.

My journey has been built through life experience, creativity, problem-solving, and a determination to keep growing no matter what season of life I was in. For years, I worked in leadership, customer support, operations, and creative design — learning how businesses really function behind the scenes and how important people are to every process.

Over time, I realized I didn't just want to support systems… I wanted to build them. I began teaching myself programming, web development, automation, and business-focused software design. What started as curiosity quickly became passion. Today, I create websites, operational platforms, and custom business tools designed to help small businesses and organizations work smarter, communicate better, and grow with confidence.

I believe technology should feel human. Too many businesses are overwhelmed by complicated systems or generic solutions that don't truly fit how they operate. My approach combines creativity, strategy, and real-world business understanding to build solutions that are practical, polished, and personal.

At this stage in my life, I'm not chasing trends — I'm building legacy. Every project I take on is an opportunity to create something meaningful, useful, and lasting for the people I serve.
